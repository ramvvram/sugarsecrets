Tender Care




FACIAL TIPS

1. Avoid wearing make up for 24 hours

2. Avoid the sun for 24 hours especially if the treatment included a glycolic peel

3. Visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to see fantastic results and as a preventative measure

WAXING TIPS

1. Take extra care of the waxed area especially within the first 24 hours

2. Avoid sunbathing, sun bed treatments and very hot baths for 48 hours as the waxed areas will be sensitive to heat

3. Avoid using exercise and swimming for 24 hours after waxing as this could cause possible infection in the hair follicle

4. Avoid using exfoliating products for 24 hours after waxing as they might further irritate your skin

EYELASH EXTENSION AFTER-CARE TIPS

1. Do not wash eyes, taking a shower, swim or going to spa and avoid crying, sweat, and steam for 6 hours after eyelash extension application

2. Avoid rubbing and pulling your eyelashes

3. Avoid oil-based and waterproof mascara. Regular mascara is not recommended for use as it will dissolve the adhesive with the lashes. When using mascara, please use the ones exclusively for eyelash extension.

4. Avoid any creams, eye makeup removers, lotions, sunscreens or cleansers containing oils. Oils dissolve the adhesive and loosen the attaching part of eyelash extensions. 5. Do not use a mechanical eyelash curler on your eyelash extensions.

6. Do not sleep with your face down.

7. Comb eyelash extensions with an eyelash comb or a screw brush.

8. Do not get strong water pressure on your eyes directly.

9. Dry eyelash extensions by cool air after taking a shower.

10. Be careful not to get entangled cotton fibre on eyelash extensions.

WAXING

Beautiful porcelain smooth skin requires commitment, not a miracle. A well-waxed skin surface is one of life’s great secrets to beautiful healthy skin.
Waxing removes hair from the root, so it takes a longer time for hair to grow again anywhere from 3-8 weeks, depending on the individual speed of hair growth.

No Skin Damage

In contrast to other methods of hair removal like hair removing creams and shaving, waxing does not cause cuts, pigmentation, or badly smelling skin. No harsh chemicals are present in wax, so the skin is saved from the damaging effect.

Finer and Softer Body Hair

The hair that grows after waxing is of a finer and softer texture, not rough and hard as after shaving.

Double Function of Hair Removal and Exfoliation

Waxing leaves the skin soft, smooth and rejuvenated because it also removes a layer of dead skin cells from the surface of the skin, and thus, indirectly serves the function of exfoliating the skin as well.

Waxing Diminishes Hair Growth

After repeated sessions of waxing, you will notice a much sparser hair growth, and hair growth may even stop eventually. In contrast, shaving stimulates hair growth, because it removes hair at the surface only, not at the root.
Avoid the chore of shaving and tweezing with the long lasting benefits of waxing! Choose from a variety of services for weeks of smooth skin!
